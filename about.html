About FoodLink AI

We are building a smarter, more compassionate food rescue network in Singapore.

FoodLink AI was created to solve a simple but urgent challenge: good food should not go to waste when families, shelters, and community groups need support.

Our platform brings together food providers and community organisations through a modern digital experience that is fast, transparent, and built for real-world impact.

Our mission

To reduce food waste and improve food access by connecting surplus food with those who need it most.

Our approach

We use digital tools, AI support, and thoughtful coordination to make redistributing food simple and reliable.

Our community

We work alongside restaurants, bakeries, supermarkets, charities, shelters, and community events.

Why this matters

Food waste and food insecurity can exist side by side. FoodLink AI gives organisations a practical way to redirect safe, edible food before it is discarded.

From community kitchens to emergency food drives, our platform helps create a more resilient and compassionate food ecosystem for Singapore.

Reduce waste

Keep good food in circulation instead of sending it to landfill.

Support families

Built for real communities
